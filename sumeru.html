The Dendro Archon

Lesser Lord Kusanali, also known as Nahida, is the God of Sumeru. Nahida is the youngest Archon out of all the 7 Archons which is why she is depicted as a child. Nahida is also the most intelligent archon as she is also known as the God of Wisdom. She gives her wisdom to her people and she mostly observes human behavior as she wants to know the motifs and morals of people.

Dendro

Dendro is Greek for Tree. Dendro in-game is the newest element added and is also one of the most powerful elements. Dendro is dependent on elemental reactions in order to unleash its raw power. Dendro can be used for DPS and Sub-DPS but it is more towards as a support element that with the right team, can also be a DPS element when needed

About Sumeru

Sumeru is known as the Nation of Wisdom. This region is inspired by a mix of Middle Eastern, South Asian and Northern African Cultures. The name Sumeru means Mount Meru/ Excellent mountain. Currently, it is the biggest region in the game as it has a rainforest and desert in one region
