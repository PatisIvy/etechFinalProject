The Electro Archon

The Raiden Shogun, also known as the God of Eternity, is the archon that rules over Inazuma. The Raiden Shogun herself is a perfect puppet that the real Electro Archon, Ei, created to rule over her nation. The Raiden Shogun rules Inazuma with an Iron Fist and will do anything to protect her people. All of the fighting stance in Inazuma was derived from the Raiden Shogun as she is one of the more aggressive archons. Raiden Shogun as a playable character, can be run as a DPS (Damage per Second) Unit or a Sub-DPS, making her very flexible in teams

Electro

Electro is a core word that refers to electricity. Even though there are no electronic devices in genshin, Electro is a really great DPS and support element. Electro is a very nice element as it has a lot of elemental reactions and the playable characters who wield this element, have very good kits that makes use of Electro in its fullest potential

About Inazuma

Inazuma is also known as the Nation of Eternity, and is inspired by Japan. The name Inazuma means lightning or lightning bolt. Inazuma is a nation that is made of multiple islands and is the only region (as of May 2023) that is disconnected to the other regions in the game.
