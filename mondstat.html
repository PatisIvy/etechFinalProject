The Anemo Archon

Every nation in Genshin Impact is Ruled by a God/Archon, who represent the different elements present in the game. As of May 2023, 4 out of the 7 Nations have been released with their respective God. Barbatos is the God of Anemo (Wind), however he is much known as Venti. Barbatos doesn’t rule Mondstadt in a typical fashion, instead he lets his people rule the nation themselves while he provides the protection they need. Venti, as a playable character, is a support that groups enemies together in order for them to not stray far from the player.

Anemo

The element “Anemo” came from the Greek word “anemos” which means wind. The Anemo element in-game is very powerful as its element reaction,swirl in a nutshell ,increases other element's damage to enemies. This element is mostly considered to be a support element

About Mondstadt

Mondstadt or The City of Freedom, is Genshin Impact’s equivalent to Germany. The name “Mondstadt” also means Moon City in German. The city itself is implied to be heavily influenced by Germany and Switzerland as the in-game location of Mondstadt references a mix of locations and culture from the 2 countries.
